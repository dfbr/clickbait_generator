The socialite, typically associated with the sophistication, jazz, and opulence of the Roaring Twenties, has suddenly become the poster boy for garden lovers and DIY enthusiasts.

And it seems like Gatsby's fervor for raking is contagious. People have started posting pictures with their rakes, using the hashtag #RakingWithGatsby. 

So, grab your rake and get ready to join the rake revolution! As Gatsby says, "It’s more than just a rake; it’s a way of life. Now, if you'll excuse me, old sport, I’ve got some raking to do!"
