# The Incredible Truth About Chickens That Will Inspire You

![](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1501703979959-797917eb21c8?ixlib=rb-1.2.1&auto=format&fit=crop&w=750&q=80)

Photo by [Andre Ouellet](https://unsplash.com/@andreouellet) on [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/s/photos/chicken)

Ever heard of the saying 'as brave as a chicken'? No? Well, that's because it doesn't exist! But hang on to your hats folks, because the incredible truth about chickens that we're about to share might just inspire you to live fearlessly and start your own revolution, much like our feathery friends!

Have you ever stopped to admire a chicken? Probably not. They're just those birds that live on a farm and provide us with our breakfast eggs, right? Wrong! Chickens are far more fascinating than you might think. For instance, did you know that chickens are capable of recognizing over 100 faces? Yes, they might have the brain size of a walnut, but they surely know who's who in their coop. So, don't you dare think they won't remember you if you forget to feed them for a day!

Photo by [Jason Leung](https://unsplash.com/@ninjason) on [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/s/photos/chicken)

Now, here's something even more incredible. Chickens, believe it or not, have a certain hierarchy known as the 'pecking order'. And it's not just a random pecking here and there; it's a well-structured system where every chicken knows its place. The last time humans tried something like this, we ended up with a 'King of the hill' chaos! So, hats off to these birds for pulling off a seamless hierarchy without the aid of a constitution or a court of law.

But wait, there's more! Chickens, despite their small size and seemingly timid nature, are brave protectors when it comes to their loved ones. Ever seen a mother hen protect her chicks? She becomes a fluffy ball of feathery fury, ready to take on any threat. If that's not courage, we don't know what is. So next time you see a chicken, don't just see it as a meal on legs. See it as an inspiration for bravery, organization, and memory management. 

So, folks, let's start the revolution. Let's make 'as brave as a chicken' a thing. And while we're at it, let's remember to appreciate these unsung heroes of the farm and the incredible truths they hold. After all, a bit of unexpected inspiration can change our lives, and who knew it could come from a coop!

So, are you ready to be as brave as a chicken? We bet you are!
