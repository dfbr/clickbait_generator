# How Cersei Lannister Stays So Dependable Every Single Day

 ![Cersei Lannister Smiling](https://www.hbo.com/content/dam/hbodata/series/game-of-thrones/character/cersei-lannister-1920.jpg)
 *Cersei Lannister in a rare moment of relaxation. Image courtesy of HBO.*

If you've ever wondered how Cersei Lannister, the formidable and unflappable queen from the popular series 'Game of Thrones,' stays so dependable every single day, you're not alone. We've managed to sneak into the queen's daily routine, and rest assured, it's not dragon's blood or wildfire involved!

Her secret? A whole lot of laughter, a pinch of whimsicality, and an unexpected passion for...you guessed it, stand-up comedy!

Our unexpected journey into the queen's life began when we spotted her, not in her usual intimidating royal robes, but rather in a cozy fleece hoodie and fluffy unicorn slippers in 'King's Landing Comedy Club.' She was chuckling away at a jest made by the club’s amateur comedian—Ser Pounce, the former pet cat of King Tommen, who's surprisingly good at mouse-related puns.

![Ser Pounce performing](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/9/93/Ser_Pounce.jpg)
 *Ser Pounce and his legendary comedic performances. Image courtesy of HBO.*

When asked about her unexpected appearance in the club, Cersei responded with a giggle, "I know, I know. It's unexpected. But the secret behind my dependability and consistent strength is actually humor. Nothing like a good jester to relieve the stress of ruling seven kingdoms!"

And it's not just watching comedy, Cersei herself is quite the comedian. Her notorious one-liners and witty comebacks are legendary in the seven kingdoms. She even shared her favorite joke with us, "Why don't dragons make good secretaries? Because they always fire their bosses!" Now, that's a queen with a sense of humor!

Cersei's love for comedy is also a testament to her belief in positivity and happiness, even in the toughest times. It is her way of dealing with stress and maintaining her unwavering dependability. So, the next time you're under pressure, take a leaf out of Cersei's book and find something to laugh about. Or, as Cersei puts it, "When you play the game of thrones, you laugh or you... get very, very bored."
